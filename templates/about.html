About Us

Brig. Gurbachan Singh Bal, Sarpanch of village Sathiala Distt. Amritsar dedicated his life to better the lot of the rural poor and the village community. For this purpose, he started Baba Gobind Senior Secondary Public School in his own spacious bungalow in village Sathiala in 1975. This school is also known as "KOTHI SCHOOL" in the local community.
The school has 50 airy rooms and its campus is spread across 3 acres of land in the natural surroundings. The school consists of 45 trained teachers and 12 non-teaching members.Smt. Nanvinderjeet Kaur M.A., M.Phil,B.Ed. is the Principal of the school.

The affairs of the school are managed by Baba Gobind & Brig. G.S. Bal Trust.

The school is specially catering to the needs of the economically weaker sections of the society.
S. Satwant Singh, resident of Sathiala village and now residing in USA, whose aim is to help poor and weaker sections of the village, donates Rs. 50,000 for the fee, books and uniform of 104 poor students of the village.

Many VIP's and important personalities have attended functions and gatherings a the school. S. Sarabjit Singh D.C., S. Manmohan Singh (Ex. M.L.A.), Ranjit Singh Brahmpura cabinet minister, S.D.M Baba Bakala, Tehsildar Baba Bakala, Manjit Singh MLA, R.S. Sujlana, Chairman Punjab Public Service Commission have graced many occassions in the school.

Baba Gobind & Brig. G.S. Bal Trust

Baba Gobind Senior Secondary Public School is run by Baba Gobind and Brig. G.S. Bal Trust. The trust was headed by Brig. G.S. Bal as the President of the Trust from 1975 to 1990. After his death, Col. Joginder Singh Bal was the President from 1990 to 2013. S. Gurpal Singh, Retd. General is the present President of the committee.
Maj. Gen. Gurpal Singh Bal, son of Brig. Bal, has now taken the charge of the school as the President of the school committee. Gen. Bal has a vast experience having supervised the functioning of many schools in his vast army carrier spanning 35 years. He is currently the Dean of Student Welfare at Amity University Gurgaon, mansar. He is using his rich post and experience to run the school, in an efficient manner. No wonder, Baba Gobind Singh Senior Secondary School is now one of the leading schools in the District.
