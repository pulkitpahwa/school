Facilities

We at Baba Gobind and Brig. Bal Senior Secondary Public School, believe in all-round and holistic development of a child and we provide a pleothera of facilities to our students for their complete development in all aspects.

    School Assembly

  • To inculcate moral values
  • To motivate students towards their Religion
  • Theme based class assembly

    Laboratories

  • For enhancing scientific aptitude
  • Well designed and well equipped labs for Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Maths etc.
  • Air conditioned computer labs

    Seminars and workshops

  • Career Talks acquaint students with latest trends and options
  • Frequent in-service workshops for staff to update their skills
  • Regular grooming session for students and teachers

    Library

  • Well stocked school library with rich reference section
  • Wide range of books ranging from fiction, Science, Encyclopedia & general reading

    Activity Rooms

  • For kindling creativity with special emphasis on creative and expressive arts.
  • Quality staff for imparting soft skills

    Infirmary

  • Regular medical check up of all students twice a year by qualified Doctors
  • First aid facility available in case of emergency
