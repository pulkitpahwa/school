3 Acre campus

The school runs in the bungalow of Brig. Gurbachan Singh Bal,former Sarpanch of the village. The school is dedicated to poor and under-priviledged children of the village. The school is also known as "KOTHI SCHOOL" in the locality.

Computer Education

We believe in an all-round development of students. In present era, a person without computer education is hardly recognised. We at the school,provide regular classes for the computer training.

Cultural Events

Brig. Bal and Baba Gobind Trust School regularly organizes various cultural events and many religious festivals are also celeberated.

Achievements
