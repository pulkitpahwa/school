Our School

Brig. Bal and Baba Gobind Trust School was established in 1975 in Sathiala village, district Amritsar in the bungalow of Brig. Gurbachan Singh Bal, Sarpanch of the village. The school is dedicated to poor and under-priviledged children of the village. The school is also known as “KOTHI SCHOOL” in the locality.
The school totally runs on donations with the mission to educate each and every child of the village.

Our Mission

Education is the basic right and we believe that it should be the most important human right after food, speech and shelter. We here at the KOTHI SCHOOL provides the student the best possible education along with the basic facilities like Clean drinking water, seperate toilets for boys and girls and regular medical checkup.
Our mission is to motivate each and every children of the village to education and provide them education at a very nominal price so that they can lead a better life.
