Our School

Baba Gobind and Brig. Bal Senior Secondary Public School was established in 1975 at village Sathiala, District Amritsar in the bungalow of Brig. Gurbachan Singh Bal, Sarpanch of the village. The school is dedicated to the poor and under-priviledged children. The school is also known as The “KOTHI SCHOOL” in the locality.
The school runs entirely on donations and a very nominal fee is charged from the students.

Our Mission

Education is a basic right and we do believe that it is the most important factor in the development of a child after food, and shelter. We here at the KOTHI SCHOOL provide the students the best possible education along with basic facilities such as Clean drinking water, toilets for boys and girls and regular medical checkups.
Our misssion is to provide quality education to every child of the village so that they become responsible and responsive citizens of the country.
